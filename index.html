This project was my second financial-related project at the Hanaro Bootcamp, where I collaborated within a team of 7 to develop a mobile web platform allowing the sending of pocket money with attached photos & messages to family members and creating savings accounts for children while encouraging family engagement through family feeds and video calls. I contributed as a frontend developer using Next.js, TypeScript, and TailwindCSS languages to develop the pages. The UI/UX were done through the Figma tool to ensure intuitive and accessible mobile experience. To enhance development and maintainability, we utilized Zustand for state management and Storybook for UI component management. Real-time features were implemented using WebSocket for instant notifications, while GetStream.io enabled seamless video call functionality amongst users of the platform. Additionally, we integrated OpenAI to provide AI-powered conversation topic suggestions during video calls, based on the details of the participants of the call to enhance communication between family members.
